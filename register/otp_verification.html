Steps to complete the Organization On-boarding Process :
  1. Please Select your Organization name
  2. Enter the mobile number of the Nodal Officer
  3. Please enter the one time password to verify the information submitted
  4. OTP has been sent to the nodal officer's mobile number / email


Note:
a. After the verification process of the submitted form is complete, you will receive an email with your account details.

b. In case of any difficulty please email us at helpdesk-attendance[at]gov[dot]in .
